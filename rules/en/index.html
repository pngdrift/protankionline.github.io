Game Rules

Effective date: December 30, 2021

 

This document is the rules of the game ProTanki.

 

-These rules are common for all users. The administration of the project is responsible for compliance with the rules.

-Please note that the ProTanki administration reserves the right to restrict user access at any time without explanation. The game rules can be changed at any time without warning users. User automatically agrees with the rules by registering his account.

'List of possible restrictions:'
#Blocking (temporary/permanent) in-game
#Blocking chat (temporary/permanent) in-game
#Nulling of the game account
#Other restrictions
-Project administrators and developers have exclusive rights, have the right to fully or partially not comply with the rules of the game and can act at their discretion in any situation. Other representatives of the administration are subject to the rules of the project and internal rules.
-Administrators and moderators reserve the right to set penalties that differ from those given in the rules.
-Administration reserves the right to refuse assistance if your question/request is worded incorrectly or unclear.

'General Provisions'.
1.1 By registering on the project, you confirm your agreement to these rules.
1.2 Ignorance of the rules does not exempt you from responsibility.
1.3 Administration has the right to amend these rules at any time.
1.4 Administration is not obliged to return the game resources lost as a result of bugs, errors, rollbacks and other unforeseen cases.
1.5 The owner of the account is the person who registered the account on the server.
1.6 It is not allowed to transfer or sell your account to anyone.
1.7 You are obliged to keep the data of your account confidential. The safety of the account lies entirely on you.
1.8 The administration does not compensate for any damages caused by users who have partially or completely gained access to your account.
1.9 Responsibility for actions on your account performed by third parties lies with you.
1.10 Money used to replenish the balance of the game, are voluntary donations and are not refundable.
1.11 Administration of the project reserves the right to deny you the use of the project at any time.

'Gameplay'
2.1.1 Inactivity for more than 5 minutes and not allowed more than twice in a row in team battles is prohibited.
'Penalty: Kick.*'
2.1.2 Passive game behavior - the user is strictly forbidden to show full or partial "passive" behavior. This behavior means the user`s behavior that does not comply with the rules of the game modes (interfering with the team, taking a place in the battle, taking crystal boxes without participating in the main stage of the game mode).
'Penalty: Kick, chat blocking from 1 hour to 1 day for the first offense.'
2.1.3 Actions aimed at causing interference to the team for which the user is fighting in team modes (sabotage) are forbidden. Among the following actions are considered to be sabotage: fighting against one`s own team, intentionally choosing weak weapons, frequent pauses, simulating problems with the Internet connection, fighting in the interest of the opposing team (collusion), being in the battle from several accounts*, intentionally ignoring opponents, alliances and so on.
Multaccounting - it`s forbidden to have several accounts of the same player in the same or different battles at the same time. (Applies to all game modes, except for "Parkour")
'Penalty: Kick, chat blocking from 1 hour to 1 day for the first violation.'
2.1.4 Pumping is prohibited. By pumping we mean, among other things: collusion, leading to a significant advantage in favor of one of the teams in team modes; battle between accounts of the same user; battle with players who collect crystals and do not hit back;
'Penalty: Kick, chat blocking from 1 hour to 1 day for the first offense.'

2.1.5 It is forbidden to use bugs, errors, bugs and defects in the game and site. About the bugs found should be reported to the administration of the project.
@Punishment: a fine of all game achievements (from 10%) and/or blocking of the account *.
2.1.6 The use and distribution of software that provides advantages over other players is prohibited: cheats, programs for automating the gameplay (including bots, clickers, keyboard and mouse macros) It is forbidden to intercept game data, modify them, and perform any other actions not covered by in-game functionality.
@Punishment: Blocking of the account.
2.1.7 Cheating players in order to enrich themselves is forbidden. Any fraudulent actions aimed at stealing/theft/burglary of other gamers accounts are forbidden.
'Penalty: Chat Blocking from 1 hour to 1 week for the first offense*.' #Recidivism - blocking of the account.
2.1.8 Actions aimed at obtaining the password, other account details or other personal information of any other user, including in the form of extortion with threats of moral, physical or technical abuse, are prohibited
'Penalty: Blocking of chat from 1 hour to 1 day for the first offense.'

'Communication**'.
3.1.1 All kinds of insults against other game users, administrators, project assistants and others are forbidden;
'Penalty: Blocking of chat from 5 minutes to 1 day for the first offense.'
3.1.2 It is forbidden to post information or symbols that offend or disparage the feelings of believers, lead to hate, hostility, racial, ethnic or ethnic hatred, intolerance (including insults and nicknames based on racial, national, ethnic, religious characteristics), as well as provoking interlocutor to these actions;
'Penalty: Blocking chat from 7 days, possibly blocking the account.'
3.1.3 The use of obscene language (foul language), profanity, other profanity or coarse words (expressions), not related to obscene language, including in a veiled form, is prohibited;
'Penalty: Blocking of chat from 5 minutes to 1 day for the first offense.'
3.1.4 Flooding, flaming, spamming are forbidden and also CAPS LOCK is not allowed;
'Penalty: Blocking of chat from 5 minutes to one day for the first offense*.'
3.1.5 Begging in any form, including requests for money transfers, transfer of game valuables, crystals from the Project Administration and other persons is forbidden;
'Penalty: Chat Blocking from 5 minutes to a day for the first offense*.'
3.1.6 Intrusive discussion of actions and/or inactions of the Project Administration and its assistants is forbidden.
'Penalty: Blocking of the chat room from 5 minutes to 24 hours for the first offense.'

3.1.7 Dissemination of any personal information of employees of the Administration, project assistants and third parties, including home addresses, phone numbers, passport data, is prohibited. (if such information is not publicly available)
'Penalty: Blocking of chat from 5 minutes to a day for the first offense*.'
3.1.8 Dissemination of any false information about the game, the Project Administration, Users and other persons (slander) is forbidden.
'Penalty: Blocking chat from 1 hour, possibly blocking the account.'
3.1.9 Threats against users, the Administration or other persons.
'Penalty: Blocking of the chat room from 5 minutes to 1 day for the first offense.'
3.2 All forms of discrimination based on gender, age, religion, and other characteristics is prohibited;
'Penalty: Blocking of chat from 1 hour to one day for the first offense.'
3.2.1 It is forbidden to post information about how to commit suicide, calls to commit suicide, violence, other actions that pose a threat to life or health.
'Penalty: Chat blocking from 1 hour to 1 day for the first offense.'

'Other'.
4.1.1 Any attempt to pass yourself off as a former or current employee of the Administration or Project Assistant (moderator, editor, etc.), including sending messages on behalf of the Administration or moderators, is forbidden;
' Penalty: Blocking of chat from 5 minutes to 1 day,* possibly blocking the account.'
4.2.3 It is forbidden to create battles with unacceptable names. Must not be of an offensive nature, include profanity, including in a veiled form.
' Penalty: Blocking of chat from 1 hour,* possible blocking of the account.'
4.1.2 The nickname (game login) must comply with the following standards:
- must not be offensive;
- Must not be visually similar to the nicknames of the current and former employees of the Administration and project assistants.
@Penalty: blocking of the account with a possibility to change the nickname through support service.
4.1.3 It is forbidden to use multiple accounts in order to bypass the ban, blocking chat, and bypass other restrictions for one player.
@Penalty: blocking of the account, possibly blocking by IP.
4.1.4 Third-party advertising is prohibited.
' Penalty: Blocking chat from 1 hour to a day,* possibly blocking the account.'
4.1.5 It is forbidden to post materials of pornographic and erotic nature or links to them.
' Penalty: Blocking of chat from 1 hour to one day,* possibly blocking the account.'
4.1.6 Direct or indirect promotion of drugs and alcohol is prohibited.
' Penalty: Blocking of chat from 1 day,* possible account blocking.'
4.1.7 Distribution of any confidential information about the game and plans for its development.
@ Penalty: Blocking of the account.

4.1.8 Actions, the purpose of which is to gain access to confidential user data - logins and passwords (phishing), are prohibited;
- Penalty: Permanent blocking of the chat, possibly blocking the account.
4.1.9 It is forbidden to post links to third party resources, disguised as official game resources;
- Penalty: Permanent blocking of the chat, possibly blocking of the account.
4.2.0 It is prohibited to post links to sites/hosting sites containing third-party software (cheats, programs, clients etc), links to sites containing software that modify game functionality or advertise as such;
- Punishment: permanent blocking of chat, possibly blocking of the account.
4.2.1 Other cases of fraud, aimed at the mass theft of accounts is prohibited.
- Penalty: Permanent blocking of the chat, possibly blocking of the account.
4.2.2 In-game purchases are non-refundable unless there is a software error. In any other case, a refund made bypassing the functionality of the game or the gaming site is considered fraud.
- Blocking of the account.

'Notes'.
* If you repeatedly violate these rules, the penalty increases for each subsequent violation.
** Punishment for violation of any item of this section of the rules can be replaced by a single warning (at the discretion of the moderator).
*** Karma is a list of violations previously committed on your account. Each of your violations of the chat and/or game is saved in your personal karma. When issuing punishment, first of all, there is an assessment of your karma, if karma is neglected, the punishment can be very different from the minimum term.

Statute of Limitations - 1 week
The statute of limitations is the length of time a player who has committed a violation can be penalized.

If you see that someone violates the rules, you can notify any of the active moderators through a personal message in discord (the list of activities can be seen by the role @game moderator. In a personal message to the moderator must clearly indicate the nature of the violation and be sure to attach a screenshot of the chat, which clearly shows the violation.

-The administration manages the Game at its sole discretion, but listens to the opinion of the community.

-Use of the services of the Game is made by the Player (user) on their own and on an "as-is" basis, that is, the administration of the Game is under no circumstances liable to the Player for any direct and/or indirect damages that the Player may incur in connection with the receipt of the game service or inability to obtain such service.

-The rules are being progressively amended and updated.

-These rules can be changed by the Administration without any prior notice. The player will check these rules at least once every seven days (i.e. weekly). If there are no changes made during the mentioned periods and the Player is still using the published rules, the Player is considered to be acquainted with the current edition of the Rules as they were modified during that period of time.

-Respectfully, ProTanki team.
